Turon Recipe

Sweet banana and jackfruit spring rolls fried to perfection, a delightful snack!

Ingredients:
  • 4 ripe saba bananas (or plantains)
  • 1 cup jackfruit (fresh or canned, sliced thinly)
  • 1 pack spring roll wrappers (around 10-12 sheets)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (for frying)
Instructions:
  1. Peel the bananas and cut each one into thirds.
  2. If using canned jackfruit, drain and cut into thin strips. If using fresh, cut it into small strips as well.
  3. Sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon (if using) onto a plate.
  4. Place a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface, then add a slice of banana and some jackfruit in the center.
  5. Sprinkle a little sugar mixture over the banana and jackfruit.
  6. Carefully roll the wrapper around the banana and jackfruit, folding in the sides to seal the ends.
  7. Repeat until all the wrappers and filling are used up.
  8. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the spring rolls until golden and crispy, turning occasionally for even frying.
  9. Once fried, remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your crispy, sweet Turon!
