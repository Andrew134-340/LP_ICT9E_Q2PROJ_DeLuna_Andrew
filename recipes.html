Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/2 cup sweetened jackfruit
  • 1/4 cup sweetened coconut strips
  • 1/4 cup sweetened purple yam (ube)
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 scoop of ube ice cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened beans, jackfruit, coconut strips, and ube.
  2. Top with shaved ice.
  3. Pour evaporated milk over the ice.
  4. Top with ube ice cream for added flavor.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
