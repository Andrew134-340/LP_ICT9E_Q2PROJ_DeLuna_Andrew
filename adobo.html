Adobo Recipe

A savory Filipino dish made with chicken or pork marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and spices, then simmered to perfection.

Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken (or pork), cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, combine chicken (or pork), soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for best results.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated chicken (or pork) along with the marinade. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the meat is cooked through.
  4. Pour in the water and continue to simmer for another 10-15 minutes, allowing the sauce to reduce and thicken.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you prefer a stronger vinegar flavor, add a bit more vinegar and simmer.
  6. Serve the Adobo hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
