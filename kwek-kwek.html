Kwek-Kwek Recipe

Quail eggs coated in a crunchy, golden batter, perfect for a snack or appetizer!

Ingredients:
  • 12 quail eggs, hard-boiled
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust for consistency)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder (for color)
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil (for frying)
  • Wooden skewers or toothpicks (optional, for serving)
Instructions:
  1. Carefully peel the quail eggs after boiling and set them aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and turmeric powder.
  3. Gradually add water and mix until you have a smooth batter.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Dip each quail egg into the batter, ensuring it’s well-coated.
  6. Carefully place the coated quail egg into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the fried eggs from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Optionally, skewer the eggs with wooden sticks or toothpicks for easy serving.
  9. Serve hot with a side of sweet and spicy vinegar or your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy your crispy Kwek-Kwek!
