Halo-Halo Recipe

A refreshing Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, sweet fruits, beans, and topped with leche flan and ice cream.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup shaved ice (or crushed ice)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened bananas (saba)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans (or kidney beans)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka), sliced
  • 1/4 cup leche flan, cubed
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tablespoon sweetened coconut (macapuno)
  • 1 tablespoon crispy rice flakes (pinipig)
  • Evaporated milk (for topping)
Instructions:
  1. In a tall glass or bowl, add a generous amount of shaved ice or crushed ice.
  2. Layer the sweetened bananas, red beans, jackfruit, and macapuno over the ice.
  3. Add the leche flan cubes and top with ube and vanilla ice cream.
  4. Sprinkle with crispy rice flakes (pinipig) for crunch.
  5. Drizzle evaporated milk over the top of the dessert.
  6. Mix everything together before eating and enjoy the refreshing, sweet treat!
