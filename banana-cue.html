Banana Cue Recipe

A classic Filipino street snack made with caramelized bananas on skewers.

Ingredients:
  • 6 ripe saba bananas (or plantains)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (for frying)
  • Wooden skewers
Instructions:
  1. Peel the bananas and slice each into half, lengthwise if you prefer thinner pieces.
  2. Heat a large pan over medium heat and add the cooking oil.
  3. Once the oil is hot, coat the bananas in the brown sugar and place them in the pan to fry.
  4. Fry the bananas until golden brown and the sugar has caramelized, turning occasionally to prevent burning.
  5. Once the bananas are caramelized, remove them from the pan and let them cool slightly.
  6. Skewer the bananas onto the wooden sticks and serve while still warm.
