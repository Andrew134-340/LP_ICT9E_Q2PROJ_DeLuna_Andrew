Leche Flan Recipe

A rich and creamy caramel custard dessert that's a classic Filipino favorite!

Ingredients:
  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (for caramel)
Instructions:
  1. In a small saucepan, melt the sugar over low heat, stirring continuously, until it turns golden brown.
  2. Quickly pour the caramelized sugar into the bottom of a llanera or mold, tilting to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool and harden.
  3. In a mixing bowl, gently whisk the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract until well combined. Avoid creating too many bubbles.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to ensure a smooth custard.
  5. Pour the custard mixture into the prepared llanera over the hardened caramel.
  6. Cover the llanera with aluminum foil and steam over low heat for 30-35 minutes, or until the custard is set.
  7. Remove from heat and let it cool completely before refrigerating for at least 2 hours.
  8. To serve, run a knife along the edges of the mold, invert onto a serving plate, and release the flan.
  9. Enjoy your smooth and creamy Leche Flan!
