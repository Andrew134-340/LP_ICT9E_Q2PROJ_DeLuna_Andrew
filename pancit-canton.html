Pancit Canton Recipe

A flavorful stir-fried noodle dish packed with savory meats, vegetables, and soy sauce.

Ingredients:
  • 500g Pancit Canton noodles
  • 1/2 lb chicken breast, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup snow peas, sliced diagonally
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (for serving)
Instructions:
  1. In a large pot, cook the Pancit Canton noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté until fragrant and softened.
  3. Add the chicken and shrimp to the pan. Stir-fry until the chicken is cooked through and the shrimp turns pink.
  4. Add the carrots and snow peas. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir to coat the meat and vegetables evenly.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 5 minutes to combine the flavors.
  7. Add the cooked Pancit Canton noodles to the wok. Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce and the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with lemon wedges for a refreshing kick.
